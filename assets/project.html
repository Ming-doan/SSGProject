The Interview
Right time
Around June and July are the right time to conduct an interview. Because, this time is exactly match with the time that both student in secondary and high school prepare for their graduated exams. The Interview is the oppotunity to share their demand directly related to academic issues.
Neccessary
Beside the time, the interview is neccessary to make an interview because, parent and student are dive into the exam. Thus, the collected data is more exact than others.
The Show
Destination
The FPT University is our choice to be a place for the show. We will invite both student and parent in order to raise the communication among them. The show would be recored by hidden camera to analyse and prepare an advices for them. We also invite some mentor who has experiences on psychology to help them solve their own problems.
Marketing
We would like to public our video and interview to the social media. Compare to the traditional media, the modern media are used by young generations, which is our main target.
